# Design Partner Program

## What we're offering

ClearAgent is building the compliance infrastructure layer for AI agent deployments. We're looking for 5–10 design partners who are deploying AI agents in regulated environments and need a defensible audit trail before August 2026.

Design partners get:

- **Early access** to ClearAgent before public launch
- **Direct engineering access** — weekly calls, Slack channel, your issues go to the top of the queue
- **Co-development** — your use case shapes the product; named case study rights if you want them
- **Founding pricing** — locked in before we raise and reprice
- **Regulatory defense support** — we'll help you map ClearAgent's output to your specific regulator's requirements

## What we need from you

- Deployed (or about to deploy) AI agents in at least one of: financial services, healthcare, HR, legal, or government
- A compliance team or legal team with active EU AI Act exposure
- Willingness to share real-world requirements, not theoretical ones
- One technical person and one compliance/legal person as contacts

## Ideal design partner profiles

**Financial services — procurement or AP automation**
You have AI agents approving vendor payments, processing invoices, or executing trades. Your compliance team needs to prove that every AI decision above a threshold was reviewed by a human. ClearAgent is your audit layer.

**Healthcare — clinical decision support**
AI assists clinicians with diagnosis or treatment recommendations. Regulatory frameworks require logging AI recommendations and documenting when clinicians override them. ClearAgent handles the logging and override audit trail.

**HR tech — hiring or performance decisions**
AI screens candidates, flags performance outliers, or recommends compensation changes. EU AI Act high-risk classification applies. You need to prove human review of AI recommendations before adverse employment decisions.

**Legal tech — contract review or risk assessment**
AI reviews contracts, flags risk clauses, or recommends negotiating positions. Client-facing decisions need audit trails. ClearAgent logs the AI recommendation and the human sign-off.

**Government / public sector**
Public procurement, benefits eligibility, or regulatory filings assisted by AI. Maximum regulatory exposure, maximum need for defensible audit trails.
